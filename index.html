Match Algorithm

High-attraction capybaras (4–5 stars) match rarely. When they do, they respond slowly. Low-attraction capybaras match more frequently and are more likely to message first. This reflects natural capybara social dynamics.

The algorithm uses a probability curve weighted against attraction score, with randomness added to prevent predictability.
