About CapyLove V2.4.0

The world's only capybara dating platform. 1,006 real capybara images from the capy.lol image pool, scored across a 6-dimension V2.3 attraction algorithm with letter-grade classification (S through D).

503 males, 503 females. Each assigned a unique energy type, tax bracket, biome of origin, 2–4 hobbies, a simulated distance from your location, and a persona from a pool of 40 (20 per gender) ranked by potency 1–20.

Higher-grade capybaras are rarer matches, reply slower, and carry higher-potency personas. Lower-grade capybaras match often, message first, and are enthusiastically available.

Attraction Grade System

Every capybara receives a composite V2.3 score (1–99) from 6 weighted dimensions. That score maps to a letter grade displayed on their card and woven into their bio.

S  88+ A  74+ B  58+ C  42+ D  <42

Grade S capybaras match ~4% of the time. Grade D match ~93%. This reflects authentic capybara social dynamics.

V2.3.3 Scoring Dimensions

Six metrics combine to produce the composite score. Each card shows all six as live bars.

FUR
Fur Density 22%
Coat thickness, fluff rating, and wetness tolerance index. Dense fur signals robust genetics and superior thermoregulation.
WGT
Weight Score 18%
Optimal range 35–65 kg. Larger specimens signal better resource access and territory security. Chonk is virtue, within limits.
SZE
Body Size 14%
Length-to-snout proportions and muzzle width. A broader muzzle indicates superior grazing capacity and social dominance potential.
VBE
Vibe / Friendliness 12%
Inter-species tolerance score. Willingness to permit terrapin cohabitation, bird perching, and capybara group formation. Heavily weighted toward warmth.
NRG
Energy Level 20%
Rated 1–10. Peak compatibility at 6–7. Below 4 reads as inert; above 8 reads as overwhelming. Both extremes reduce long-term pairing probability.
BIO
Biome of Origin 14%
Pantanal Wetlands (96) and Llanos Savanna (92) score highest. Cloud Forest Ecuador (91) is rare and therefore valued. Caatinga Scrubland (65) scores lowest.

Persona System

40 unique personas — 20 male, 20 female — each with a potency rating 1–20. Higher-grade capybaras are weighted toward higher potency. Potency governs intensity across 9 behavioral axes:

HumorSensualFlirtyReservedAwkwardMeanNiceKindRude

P1 personas are warm and nervous. P20 personas are intense, magnetic, and cold. A Grade S capy with P20 will rarely reply. A Grade D with P2 will message you first within minutes.

Distance System

Set your city on first load and every capybara gets a simulated distance from you — seeded by their ID so it stays consistent. The Nearby filter (Discover tab) shows only capybaras within 50 miles.

Distances are simulated. Real capybaras do not own phones. Most are in South America. We are aware.
